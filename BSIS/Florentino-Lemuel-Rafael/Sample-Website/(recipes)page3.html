Lumpiang Shanghai

Ingredients:

  • ground pork

  • green onions

  • carrots

  • Onion

  • eggs

  • salt

  • garlic powder

  • parsley, minced

  • ground black pepper

  • Spring roll wrapper

  • Cooking Oil

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine all the ingredients with the pork starting with the onion, carrots, green onions, parsley, salt, ground black pepper, garlic powder, and 1 piece raw egg. Mix well.

  2. Wrap the about 1 to 2 tablespoons of the mixture in a lumpia wrapper (for proper way, see video). Do this until all the mixture are consumed.

  3. Heat the cooking oil in a deep fryer. Deep fry the lumpia for 10 to 12 minutes.

  4. Remove from the deep fryer and let the excess oil drip.

  5. Transfer to a serving plate. Serve with sweet and sour sauce

  6. Serve hot. Share and enjoy!


Malunggay Corn Soup

Ingredients:

  • white corn, shredded

  • malunggay leaves, cleaned

  • chicken broth

  • garlic, minced

  • onion, chopped

  • extra virgin olive oil

  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat a cooking pot then pour-in olive oil.

  2. When the oil becomes hot, saute the garlic and onions.

  3. Put-in the shredded corn and stir.

  4. Pour-in chicken broth and let boil. Simmer for 8 to 10 minutes.

  5. Add salt and ground black pepper then cook for 2 minutes more.

  6. Put-in the malunggay leaves. Stir and simmer for 2 minutes more.

  7. Turn-off heat and transfer to a serving bowl.

  8. Serve hot. Share and enjoy!


Beef Picadillo

Ingredients:

  • beef brisket, cut into cubes (or ground beef)

  • very ripe tomatoes, chopped

  • potatoes, cut into cubes

  • garlic, crushed

  • onion, chopped

  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a casserole, saute garlic, onion and tomatoes.

  2. Cook until tomatoes are very soft. Add the meat and about 8 cups water.

  3. Simmer until meat is almost tender. Add cubed potatoes.

  4. Continue cooking till potatoes are done. Season with salt.

  5. Serve hot. Share and enjoy!


Pork Liempo (Lechon Kawali)

Ingredients:

  • pork liempo(pork belly), cut into serving pieces

  • garlic, crushed

  • pepper or 1 tsp. peppercorns

  • salt

  • Water for boiling

  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Put pork pieces in a big pot. Cover with water. Add garlic, pepper or peppercorns and salt.

  2. Bring to a boil. Lower heat and simmer for an hour or until meat is tender. Drain.

  3. Deep-fry liempo pieces in batches until golden brown and blisters appear on skin.

  4. Drain on paper towels.

  5. Serve with chopped lechon kawali.

  6. Serve hot. Share and enjoy!


Beef Salpicao

Ingredients:

  • beef tenderloin, cut into cubes

  • Garlic cloves, chopped well

  • Butter

  • Olive Oil

  • Cooking Oil

  • Salt and pepper to taste

  • mixed vegetables (broccoli, snow peas, cauliflower or any vegetable you wish)

  • brown sugar

  • chili fakes

  • oyster sauce, Worcestershire sauce or liquid seasoning

  • paprika

Instructions:

The Side dish

  1. Steam the vegetables of your choice for about 3 minutes. Make sure that it becomes tender

  2. Place the steamed vegetables in a serving bowl

  3. Melt butter then add the melted butter in the bowl. Toss the vegetables well.

  4. Add salt and pepper to taste.

The Main dish

  1. The first thing that you need to do is to marinade the beef. Place the beef cubes in a big bowl then add in the liquid seasoning, Worcestershire sauce or oyster sauce, brown sugar, chili flakes, olive oil, minced garlic and paprika. Allow the beef to be marinated for two hours or so.

  2. Heat the wok. Wait for it to start to smoke. Once you have seen smoke, then you should start adding a generous amount of oil. Add in the minced garlic. Stir fry until it becomes fragrant. Avoid turning the garlic brown.

  3. Remove the cooked garlic from the wok but leave the garlic infused oil behind

  4. Add the beef cubes in batches. Stir cook for about five to ten minutes until the beef cubes have seared.

  5. Once all the beef cubes have been placed in the wok, add in the cooked garlic, butter and oyster sauce.

  6. Stir cook the beef cubes for about three to five minutes. Continue doing this until the sauce exhibits an oily and thick consistency.

  7. Test the taste. Add salt if necessary.

  8. Remove the beef salpicao from the wok.

  9. Served right away with rice on the side.

  10. Serve hot. Share and enjoy!
