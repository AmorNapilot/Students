CHEN LONG

Chen Long is a male badminton player from China. He is an Olympic Bronxe medalist,two-time World Champion. And two-time All England Champion. As of 17 September 2015,he is ranked NO.1 in the world, a ranking he has maintained following his ascension to the top ranking position at the end of 2014.
