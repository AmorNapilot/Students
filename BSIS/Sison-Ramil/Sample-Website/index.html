BADMINTON TRIVIA
BADMINTON

-is a reactional sport played using racquets to hit a shuttlecock across a net.

-Although it may be played with larger teams, the most common forms of the game are

"singles"(with one player per side) and "doubles"(with two players per side).

First played: 19th Century

Team members: Singles or Doubles

Equipment:

  1. Racket

  2. Shuttle Cock
