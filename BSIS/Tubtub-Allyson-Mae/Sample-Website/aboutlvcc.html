Our Humble Beginning

LA VERDAD CHRISTIAN SCHOOL was officially registered in the Securities and Exchange Commission offering PRE-SCHOOL and ELEMENTARY GRADES on May 27, 1998. LVCS started its first year of operation in S.Y. 1999-2000.

Having the Government Recognition Permit No. E-037 for Elementary, LVCS had only 50 students, and only 13 students for Pre-School with Government Recognition Permit No. E-026 s. 2000.

In 2001, LVCS decided to offer Secondary Level, with Government Permit No. S-027 s. 2001. It started with 39 High School Students, 30 from first year and only 9 second year. It was 2002, when the school had its first batch of graduating students. In 2005, La Verdad Christian School expands its horizon by adding Tertiary Level under the banner LA VERDAD CHRISTIAN COLLEGE. It started with only 154 students. In 2009 by God's will and mercy, through the initiation of our Founding Chairman and President, LVCS and LVCC formally started the FULL SCHOLARSHIP GRANT. And in 2015, La Verdad Christian College has been granted a permit to offer SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL effective School Year 2016-2017
