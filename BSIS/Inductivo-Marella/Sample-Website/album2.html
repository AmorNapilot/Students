My love my Family

Family is not an important thing. It's everything. All happy families are alike; each unhappy family is unhappy in its own way. To us, family means putting your arms around each other and being there. Family means no one gets left behind or forgotten. I think togetherness is a very important ingredient to family life. “No man has ever lived that had enough of children’s gratitude or woman’s love.” Sister is probably the most competitive relationship within the family, but once the sisters are grown, it becomes the strongest relationship. “Rejoice with your family in the beautiful land of life!” A happy family is but an earlier heaven. In every conceivable manner, the family is link to our past, bridge to our future. I've always put my family first and that's just the way it is. One of the things that binds us as a family is a shared sense of humor. A father is a man who expects his son to be as good a man as he meant to be. It was my father who taught me to value myself. He told me that I was uncommonly beautiful and that I was the most precious thing in his life. It is easier for a father to have children than for children to have a real father. Love and fear. Everything the father of a family says must inspire one or the other.
