LA VERDAD CHRISTIAN COLLEGE WON SILVER MEDALS

Mr. Rodrigo Galura III, a graduate of Associate in Computer Programming and taking up Bachelor of Science in Information System competed for Web Design and Mr. Jericho Serrano, a graduate of International Cookery competed for Cooking and they were both awarded as silver medalist in the said competition.

The school, computer expert Mr. Igee Martinez and cooking coaches Ms. Rhen Sasondoncillo and Chef Julian Cenon gave their full support in order to won the competition. Mr. Igee Martinez said that they only have four days preparation for the focus of competition compared to other institutions and colleges who were participants every year although the organizers together with the experts and coaches have their meeting for the upcoming event that time. After all, we congratulate our fellow La Verdarians for being the pride of our school.

LVCC's Blue Achors with the SG Officers and Mr. Albert Soriano.

LVCC Blue anchors scored as they won 8 golds, 16 silver and 9 bronze medals in different categories. Despite of the hardships and crucial moments experienced by the players they still gave pride to La Verdad. This was the first time of LVCC to join the event. With Gods help and mercy some of the anchors were given a chance to be part on the upcoming PRISAA Nationals this April.
