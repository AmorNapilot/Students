MINOR OFFENSES

1. Non-submission of letter of excuse for absence upon returning to school.
2. Habitual absences /tardiness.
3. Non-submission of report card and other slips/forms after deadline..
4.Bringing unnecessary objects to school (Confiscation is part of the .penalty).
5. Engaging in petty quarels..
7. Cutting classes..
8. Unauthorized haircut..
9. Inappropriate appearance not in line with the prescribed grooming of a La Verdarian such as dyed/colored hair, punk attire, earings, chains and use of cosmetics..
10. Wearing slippers inside the shool premises is striclty prohibited..
11. Violation of the cellular phone policy. (Cellular phones are not allowed).
12. Unauthorized use of the classroom equipment..
13. Non-attendance in school activities without valid reason..
14. Indecent behavior/language in the classroom , during flag ceremony, official meeting and other activities..
15. Habitual disregard of assignments in class, projects, and other requirements (three times a week is equivalent to one minor offense).
16. Unauthorized staying in the classroom after school hours without valid reason..
17. Improper use of the comfort room. (Taking a bath is not allowed).
18. Bringing of unnecessary objects to school. (toys, cards, etc.).
