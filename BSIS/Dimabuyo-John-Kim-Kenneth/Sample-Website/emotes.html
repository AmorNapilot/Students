Emotes

Players may express themselves through chat (default Y) or use (U) to whisper. Whispering looks the same as chat, but the word "Whisper" is displayed before it. Whispered messages can only be seen by players who are in the vicinity of the speaker. Emotes are triggered by entering the commands in chat such as /sad or /cry, /annoyed, /bye, /angry, /happy, /kiss, /bonesaw, /dance, /wave, /facepalm, /joy, and /pose, which will trigger an animation by the player accordingly.
