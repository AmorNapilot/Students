Skins

A new feature of Don't Starve Together is the option to change the physical appearance of each playable character with alternate skins. They will serve to give a sense of individuality to players who are using the same character in the same server. Skins are obtainable through random drops while playing the game, up to 4 per week, resetting around 1pm PST on Thursday. Skins act more like non-functional clothing, as they are for aesthetic use only. The player may be able to customize pants, shirts, shoes, and gloves for their character. Some Skins also change the general appearance of the character, but they are character-specific and drop very rarely. All clothing type Skins, however, are not character or gender specific. This means that, for example, any character can wear a skirt. Certain items such as the normal Backpack will also have alternate skins, but drops for those will be rarer.
