The Colombian Government Enterprise Architecture Framework

Based on the article it is said that the Colombian Government is not lacking when it comes to Information technology Management in fact it is being implemented in public agencies, but despite standardization in a national level, the maturity of the ITM does not reach the level in which it benefits the state’s objectives and goals, according to the graph, the Colombian state’s EA framework is mostly in the 'Business Silos' section, while some are located in the standardized section they are still outweighed by the former.
A business Silo is a term in which it brings fewer benefits for the administration. Therefore there is a need for standardization to improve the performance of the administration with the purpose of better cooperation between departments and faster and more efficient publication of higher quality information for the public all for the sake of achieving the national goals.
The Ministry of Information Technologies and Communications has been working on the construction of the Colombian Government Enterprise Architecture Framework (CGEAF), which corresponds to a set of principles, guidelines and standards for information technology management in the State, and available to the Colombian public agencies to be used as a strategic direction, for the construction of the institutional, regional and national enterprise architectures.
With the implementation of said CGEAF it can improve the Colombian government’s efficiency and transparency through proper management of Information technology strategy etc.
